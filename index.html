ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Paulo Victor, I'm a master student of Integrated Design with expertise in Industrial design since 2008. My experience relies on strategic design and mostly on industrial design. My core competencies are the care with the design process and the pursuit of a minimal design.

I have product design skills such as sketching, 3D modeling , 3D rendering and digital illustration .

I am seeking opportunities to develop products from the ground up or to enrich a pontual phase of any design process.

Also, I'm the founder of LESMO, a design brand of downloadable furniture.
